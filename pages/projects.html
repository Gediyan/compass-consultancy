Compass AEPED Establishment

Compass AEPED is an established urban and regional planning consultancy with over 15 years of expertise in the field of planning and development. Headquartered in Addis Ababa, we are committed to shaping sustainable urban environments

Innovative Engineering Solutions

We deliver cutting-edge architectural and engineering solutions tailored to Ethiopia's unique context. Our team combines international standards with local expertise to create functional, sustainable spaces.

Sustainable Development Approach

Our approach integrates environmental responsibility with urban development. We prioritize green spaces, energy efficiency, and community-focused designs in all our projects.
